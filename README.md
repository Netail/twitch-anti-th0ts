# Twitch Anti Th0ts

Somewhere in 2023, Twitch has changed their ToS regarding nudity in such a way that a flood of streamers began to show more of their body with (usually) the intention of selling their paid content. This resulted in the homepage & entire categories being filled twitch streamers selling their bodies in sexual ways.

This chrome extension tries to blur the stream previews of these Twitch streamers, handy for when you're streaming yourself and are browsing through Twitch.
